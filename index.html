Samagra foundation aims to provide water , health, education, livehood, micro enterpretation, physical infrastructure at village.

In the progressive world that we live in today, education to all is a must. We at Samagra Foundation try to do our part in making India a country with 100% literacy rate. We have paired up with various orphanages and educational institutes to promote our initiative. Many school in the remote areas of the country lack proper teaching faculty, thanks to the digital era we live in the enthusiastic teachers and instructors in our organization can reach out to the students eager to learn in any corner of the country. We have collaborated with many teachers and educators from varied departments to fill in for the shortage of staff and faculty in the schools and institutes in the remote areas of the country.


We promote the idea of education for all in all our initiatives and that the youth of today are leaders of tomorrow. Providing meals to the children of the orphanages and schools in our proximity and encouraging the parents to send their children to school is a major focus of ours.


We progressing constantly and working hard to fulfil this endeavor of ours by expanding our reach and thanks to all our benefactors for giving wings to what was once just a dream.
